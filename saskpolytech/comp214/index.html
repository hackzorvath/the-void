COMP 214

Project Management Fundamentals

You will develop project management skills that will be applied to authentic projects. You will study industry standards and methodologies and learn about the resources available to project managers. You will explore tools and techniques used throughout the project life cycle, from initiation to closing, applying theory to real-world scenarios.

Phases:

  • ➡️ 1: Initiation — Introduction to Project Thinking
  • ➡️ 2: Planning — Scoping and Scheduling with Milestones
  • ➡️ 3: Execution — Communication and Risk Management
  • ➡️ 4: Closing — Presentation & Reflection
