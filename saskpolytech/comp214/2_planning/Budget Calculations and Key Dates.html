Time Budget Calculations

During the Initiation phase of your project, you will calculate the amount of time available to work on this project, this is called capacity.

Capacity = (C + H - L) * T - P

C = Total available class hours from start date to presentation date

H = Total available homework hours agreed upon by the team

L = Total lost hours due to projected potential absences

P = Planned or known absences (holidays, travel plans, weddings, etc.)

T = Team size

Note: For calculating class hours, consider that this class is 4 hours per week and runs for approximately 15 weeks.
